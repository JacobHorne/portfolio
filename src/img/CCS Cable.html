Describe the company and the service it provides

CCS, Creative Computer Solutions is a full service cable provider who specializes in Structured Cabling, IT Management, Voip Phone Systems, and Security Systems.

Who is the target audience for the site? What information/content is the audience looking for?

TThe primary audience for CCS are contractors and developers in the Columbia, SC area who see a full service IT provider. The audience is looking for easy to find information on the four service categories and a way to easily contact that provider.

What are the company’s primary goals/objectives?

The previous website for CCS was dated, wasn’t responsive, and not very appealing. CCS seeks a more modern looking site that is responsive and represents a more professional brand. They hope this will help them drive more business being in the early stages of their company.

Describe the Company’s Brand, Personality, and Culture

The brand for CCS is that of all in one solution company. The company prides itself on being punctional, professional, and customer satisfaction.
